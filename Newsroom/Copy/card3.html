KFC PUNTA ARENAS:THE END OF THE WORLD’S BUCKET IS HERE!

October 17, 2024

We are thrilled to share a monumental achievement for KFC LA&C—the grand opening of KFC Punta Arenas, located in the breathtaking region of Chilean Patagonia! Tucked away in the southernmost part of the globe, Punta Arenas is known for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and crazy cold weather!

As the temperatures dip to bone-chilling lows, our finger-lickin’ good chicken is ready to bring some warmth to this unique corner of the world by celebrating the opening with a very special “End of the World Bucket”!Ready for more…?

In celebration of International Day of Sign Languages, KFC is taking a monumental step in the hospitality industry, as it becomes the first QSR in India to launch a Sign Language training program for 100% of its employees. The first-of-its-kind training is required to be undertaken by all 17,000+ KFC employees, who are spread across 240+ cities in 1200+ restaurants, as well as those working in the brand’s corporate offices.

​Punta Arenas is now home to a new KFC, serving up that signature ‘krunchiness’ we all know and love! Located inside the “Mall Espacio Urbano Pionero,” we are ready to deliver our irresistible flavors and create an unforgettable experience for our guests, even in the coldest of weathers!

A huge shoutout to our franchisee, Grupo Serrano, and the LA&C team for this incredible accomplishment! Your dedication and hard work have brought KFC to the end of the world, and we couldn't be prouder.

Let’s continue spreading ‘krunchiness’ across the globe… One bucket at a time!
