WHERE WE DISH IT UP

30,000 KFCs and growing

AFRICA

  • Year: 1971
  • 1,400+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: Since 2009, KFC South Africa has served more than 250 million meals with a bold goal of serving 500 million meals by 2030.

ASIA

  • Year: 1966
  • 4,000+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: Our team in Taiwan shared KFC’s very first AI-created team member Kala by in 2024.

AUSTRALIA

  • Year: 1968
  • 800+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: More than 90% of KFC team members in Australia are under the age of 25, making us one of the largest employers of young Aussies.

CANADA

  • Year: 1955
  • 4,000+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: The Colonel’s trademark white suits were tailored in Toronto, Ontario.

CHINA

  • Year: 1987
  • 10,000+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: KFC's menu in China includes fried chicken, egg tarts, congee, and steamed dumplings.

EUROPE

  • Year: 1965
  • 2,100+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: KFC Europe serves customers from more than 35 countries.

India

  • Year: 1995
  • 1,100+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: In 2013, India’s first ever KFC restaurant to be managed and powered by an all-women team was launched.

LATIN AMERICA & CARRIBEAN

  • Year: 1973
  • 1,800+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: KFC’s famous Zinger was created in Trinidad & Tobago over 40 years ago.

MIDDLE EAST, TURKEY & NORTH AFRICA

  • Year: 1973
  • 1,500+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: KFC first launched its Rewards program in this region.

THAILAND

  • Year: 1984
  • 1,000+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: As of 2023, KFC Thailand has won the "Best Brand Performance on Social Media" award at the Thailand Zocial Awards for eight years in a row.

UNITED KINGDOM & IRELAND

  • Year: 1965
  • 900+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: KFC UK&I were the first market to launch the vegan burger in 2019.

UNITED STATES

  • Year: 1952
  • 3,500+ restaurants
  • Fun fact: The first KFC franchise restaurant was opened in Salt Lake City, Utah in 1952, not Kentucky.
