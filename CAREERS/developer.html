Bring it.

The Strategy Analyst uses strong business acumen and analytical expertise to support and bring actionable strategic insights to KFC Global's portfolio of business units (BMUs). This includes reviewing, analyzing, and drawing conclusions from BMU inputs and consolidating BMU-level data into a division-level narratives for reporting. This role also performs strategic analysis on value-added business priorities to share with KFC Global leadership and drive overall brand results.

What You'll Be Cooking Up:

Franchisee and Enterprise Risk Manager (30%)

  • Perform annual franchisee financial health submission to Yum Franchising Office through collection of scorecards from the BMUs.
  • Compile quarterly large franchisee 3C update for Yum Audit Committee.
  • Compile and maintain list of franchisees with relevant performance metrics from each BMU.

Competitive Intelligence (30%)

  • Complete top competitor financial performance analysis vs KFC on a quarterly basis.
  • Consolidate market share data through partnership with BMUs and external data providers.
  • Research, monitor, and report competitive landscape quarterly.

Strategic Planning and Analysis (40%)

  • Support Strategy Team in performing analysis as needed for growth initiatives, key meetings, and other ad hoc projects.
  • Ongoing evaluation of brand accelerators and big bets to inform resource prioritization.
